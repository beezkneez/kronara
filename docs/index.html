Features

Everything you need to
run your studio

From timesheet logging to payroll processing, Kronara handles the operational complexity so you can focus on what matters.

Timesheet Tracking

Staff log hours with date, location, time, class name, hours worked, and pay rate. Support for multiple shifts per day with an intuitive calendar view.

Commission Tracking

Track retail sales, tips, and other commissions separately from regular hours. Everything rolls into payroll automatically.

Smart Bonus System

Automatic bonus calculations based on weekly hours worked and class attendance thresholds. Configurable per-bonus exclusion lists.

Payroll & Reports

Generate PDF payroll reports, export CSV data, and auto-send reports via email. Supports weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, and monthly pay periods.

Google Calendar Sync

Import shifts directly from Google Calendar. Staff can sync their schedules automatically, reducing manual data entry.

Entry Flag System

Admins flag entries for review; staff respond with corrections. A transparent audit trail keeps everyone accountable and aligned.

Push Notifications

Configurable notifications by category: flags, pay reports, shift reminders. Keep your team in the loop without email overload.

Install as Phone App

Kronara is a Progressive Web App. Install it on any phone or tablet — no app store needed. Works offline too.

Built-in Support Chat

Staff can message support or report bugs directly from the app. Admins track issues and notify on fixes. AI chat assistant included.
